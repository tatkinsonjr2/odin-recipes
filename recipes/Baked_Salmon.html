Baked Salmon

Description

This is an easy to use and easy to cook recipe for baked salmon. Stay as healthy as possible with this great recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of boneless salmon fillet
  • tablespoon of olive oil
  • tablespoon lemon pepper seasoning
  • teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Lay salmon fillet skin-side down on a wide piece of aluminum foil
  2. Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinegar over salmon. Sprinkle lemon pepper, lemon juice, and salt on top.Fold foil up and over the fillet, tenting it at the top. Roll top down to seal salmon in foil. Transfer to a baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until salmon flakes easily with a fork, about 15 minutes.
